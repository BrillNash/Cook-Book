Valentine's Day Cake Mix Cookies

Enjoy a fun and tasty treat when you make Valentine's Day Cake Mix Cookies. With only a few simple ingredients, you can make this easy cake mix cookies recipe.

These cake mix cookies are frugal!
Maybe you have a large family like I do or maybe you need a bunch of cookies for a party. If so, this is the recipe for you!
Cake mix goes on sale all the time so stock up when it does. You can make these cookies very inexpensively.
This is the perfect recipe when you need to make a lot of quick and easy cookies without breaking the bank.

Ingredients for Valentine's Day Cake Mix Cookies

  • Strawberry cake mix
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • White Chocolate Chips

How to Make Valentine's Day Cake Mix Cookies

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine the cake mix, eggs and oil together in a large mixing bowl. (Batter will be thick but this is normal).
  3. Simply add in those yummy white chocolate chips. This really takes this recipe up a level.
    • I like to use a cookie scoop to scoop out the cookies and place on the dough balls on cookie sheets leaving about 2 inches between each cookie.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F for 8 to 10 minutes until cookies brown SLIGHTLY on the edges.
  5. Allow to cool completely and then serve. This is the hard part because they smell so amazing that you want to just eat one right away.

Tips to make valentines day cookies recipe:

  • If you are still scooping cookies with two spoons, you really need to get yourself some stainless steel scoops!
  • I have had mine for several years and I use them for all types of baking. I bought the 3 pack and used the smallest of the 3 scoops for these cake mix cookies (and almost all my cookies) and they were just the right size for adults and kids.
  • The other sizes are perfect for other recipes or meatballs and such. Trust me, they will get lots of use and are well worth the small investment.
  • It is also a good idea to use a cooling rack. Place the cookies on racks to cool completely.
  • We also line baking sheets with parchment paper. This makes cleaning large baking sheets easier as well.
  • You will save lots of time and this is one of our favorite easy recipes. It is one of the easiest dessert recipes ever.
