Strawberry Ice Cream

Rich and creamy homemade strawberry ice cream with real strawberry flavor!

Egg yolks
it’s the absolute most important ingredient to make a thick, creamy and rich ice cream.

Ingredients for Homemade Strawberry Ice Cream

  • Heavy Cream
  • Half and Half
  • Vanilla Bean Pods
  • Sugar
  • Egg Yolks
  • Freeze Dried Strawberries

How to Make Homemade Strawberry Ice Cream

  1. Combine all the ingredients except the eggs and strawberries to a saucepan over medium low heat. Heat the mixture just until bubbles form around the edges of the pan. Then remove the pan from the heat.
  2. Whisk the egg yolks in a bowl until they become pale yellow.
  3. Add some of the strawberries to a blender and blend until they are powder.
  4. Remove a few tablespoons (5ish) of the hot cream mixture and add it to the eggs one tablespoon at a time, whisking constantly.

    • PRO TIP:

      This slowly warms up the eggs without scrambling them. We don’t want that!
  5. Then slowly add the egg mixture into the hot cream mixture, whisking constantly still, and return the saucepan to the stove over medium low heat.
  6. Add the strawberry powder and whisk vigorously to combine everything.
  7. Crumble up the remaining strawberries and add them as well.
  8. Stir constantly until thickened.
  9. Remove from the heat and chill in the refrigerator until completely cool.
  10. Freeze the mixture in your ice cream maker.
  11. Scoop into an ice cream container and then place it in the freezer for several hours.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
